# TALENT_MAP

## Purpose

A general profile of the applicant’s talents and career direction.

## Mandatory blocks

1. Hero / quick formula.
2. Data completeness and confidence.
3. General conclusion.
4. Talents in working translation.
5. Strengths.
6. Risks / overload zones.
7. Suitable professional directions with percentages.
8. Controversial / low-fit directions with percentages.
9. Possible roles.
10. Suitable working environment.
11. Practical tests.
12. Final recommendation.

## Active design

Use Darya Talent Map v1.1 as the active baseline.

## Do not

- Do not add socionics.
- Do not write for HR.
- Do not make final destiny statements.
