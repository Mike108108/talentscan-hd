# VACANCY_ASSESSMENT

## Active etalon

`11_HTML_EXAMPLES/EXAMPLE_MIKHAIL_ONESUMMER_VACANCY_ASSESSMENT_V2_SELF_PRESENTATION.html`

Use this as the active structural and semantic etalon for all future vacancy-fit cards. Do not copy Mikhail / OneSummer data; copy only the block logic, applicant framing and UX structure.


## Purpose

Help the applicant evaluate a vacancy before applying or accepting an offer.

## Mandatory blocks

1. Quick answer by vacancy.
2. Fit percentage.
3. Best-fit parts of the vacancy.
4. Risk parts.
5. What to clarify with employer.
6. Red/yellow/green flags.
7. How to present yourself for this role: interview positioning, strengths to highlight, risks to neutralize, ready-to-use phrases.
8. What conditions must be fixed before accepting.
9. Final recommendation:
   - apply/look deeper;
   - good to test;
   - partly suitable;
   - only temporary bridge;
   - likely not suitable;
   - not recommended as core vector.

## Do not

Do not say “company should hire you.” Say “you should check / apply / test / avoid.”
