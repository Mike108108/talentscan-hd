# ROLE_VACANCY_COMPARISON

## Purpose

Help the applicant choose between several options.

## Comparison modes

- Current role vs vacancy.
- Vacancy A vs vacancy B.
- Current role vs several vacancies.
- All options.

## Mandatory criteria

- Career direction fit.
- Energy / sustainability.
- Meaning and motivation.
- Growth potential.
- Risk of burnout.
- Quality of environment.
- Leadership style risk.
- Money/schedule only if data is available.
- Recommendation and next step.

## Output

Use tables and clear recommendation. Avoid overexplaining.
