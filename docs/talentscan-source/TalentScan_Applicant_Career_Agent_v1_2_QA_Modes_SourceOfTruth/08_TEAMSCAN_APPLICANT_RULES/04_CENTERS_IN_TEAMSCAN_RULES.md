# Centers in Applicant TeamScan

Centers are mandatory in technical TeamScan when enough data exists.

But describe centers through applicant decisions:

- Where will the applicant feel stable?
- Where may the applicant feel pressure?
- Where can the leader amplify the applicant?
- Where can the applicant lose clarity?
- What should be written down instead of assumed?

## Key applicant framing

If composite creates energy, do not conclude that the applicant has sustainable personal energy.

Example:

“В композите может ощущаться много энергии, но это не значит, что Дарья должна жить в постоянном темпе. Перед согласием нужно проверить график, объем задач и границы доступности.”
